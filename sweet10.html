Chocolate Dessert

Ingredients:

  • 600 ml milk
  • 3 tablespoons condensed milk
  • 1 egg
  • 5 ml vanilla essence
  • 66 ml double thick fresh cream
  • 34 g cocoa powder
  • 34 g cornstarch
  • 120 g dark chocolate

For the topping

  • 200ml fresh cream

Recipe:

  1. In a sauce pan/pot: heat 515 mL milk, condensed milk, fresh cream and sifted cocoa powder. Mix until the cocoa powder has dissolved
  2. Cut the chocolate into small pieces.
  3. In a cup mix together 15 ml milk and cornstarch
  4. Beat the egg. Add the vanilla essence and cornstarch mixture to the beaten egg and whisk.
  5. When the milk mixture in the pan is steaming, add the egg mixture and mix with a whisk until it starts to thicken
  6. Add chopped chocolate into the thickening mixture and mix until dissolved.
  7. Continue to mix until it reaches a suitable thickness. (Do not stop mixing or else the mixture becomes lumpy).
  8. Remove from heat and put into dessert bowls. Let them sit until room temperature then put it in the fridge.

For the topping

  • Beat fresh cream and top the chocolate dessert with it.
  • Add your choice of berries if you want to.

Chocolate Mousse Cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 Cup castor sugar
  • 1 Cup cake flour
  • 2 Tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 Cup boiling water
  • 1/2 Cup Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract

For the topping

  • 250ml fresh cream
  • 100 grams dark chocolate
  • 30 grams chocolate chips

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Separate eggs, keep egg whites aside
  3. Add castor sugar and oil to the egg yolk and beat until creamy
  4. Sift flour, cocoa powder, salt and 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  5. Add a little flour at a time to the egg yolk mixture, whilst it's still beating and add boiling water in between.
  6. Add vanilla extract. Beat well until well combined. Keep aside.
  7. To the egg white mixture add 1 teaspoon of baking powder. Whisk until firm with stiff peaks.
  8. First carefully fold half of the egg white mixture into the batter using a whisk then carefully fold the rest of the egg whites.
  9. Pour into cupcake holders. Bake for 20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clear when poked into the cupcakes

For the topping

  1. In a double based saucepan heat up 80ml fresh cream and the 100 grams dark chocolate. Keep stiring continuously so that it does not stick to the bottom of the saucepan and burn.
  2. Once the chocolate is melted, take it off the heat and add it to the rest of the fresh cream in a bowl.
  3. Place the mixture in the fridge to cool.
  4. Once cold take it out the fridge and whisk it until thick with soft peaks (Do not over beat as it will turn into a butter consistency which we do not want)
  5. Dollop the mousse on top of the cupcake with a spoon, or use a piping bag.
  6. Sprinkle the chocolate chips on top and enjoy.

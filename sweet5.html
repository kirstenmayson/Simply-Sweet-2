Chocolate Squares

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 125ml Oil
  • 125ml cup butter milk (or 2 teaspoons white vinegar and half cup milk)
  • 125ml cup hot water

For the topping

  • 71 grams unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup icing sugar
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla essence

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. In a large bowl – whisk together sifted: flour, sugar, cocoa powder, soda, baking powder, salt
  3. Add egg, oil and butter milk – whisk well.
  4. Add hot water and whisk until smooth. Pour into prepared tin. Tap to release air bubbles
  5. Make the frosting while the cake is baking for 25 minutes

For the topping

  1. In a saucepan melt butter over medium heat.
  2. Add cocoa and whisk in.
  3. Cook it for two minutes but do not let it boil.
  4. Remove sauce pan from heat add icing sugar and mix well.
  5. Add vanilla and milk, 1 tablespoon at a time
  6. The frosting should be shiny and smooth.
  7. Pour the chocolate sauce over the warm cake. Let it cool before cutting. (Eat it warm.)

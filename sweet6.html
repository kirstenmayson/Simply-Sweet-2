Vanilla Sponge Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 Large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 125 grams icing sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla essense
  • 125 grams self raising flour

For the topping

  • 250 ml fresh cream
  • 5 tbsp apricot jam
  • Strawberries for decoration

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Lightly oil and line your cake tins with baking paper at the bottom.
  3. Beat the egg whites and cream of tartar until soft peaks form
  4. Gradually beat in 50g of icing sugar until stiff peaks form.
  5. In another bowl beat the egg yolks with the remaining icing sugar until thick and pale yellow.
  6. Add vanilla extract. Beat well until well combined.
  7. Gently fold the egg whites and flour alternately into the egg yolk mixture using a whisk or metal spoon.
  8. Spoon the batter into the prepared tins and spread eavenly.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12 minutes or until lighly golden brown with a firm centre.
  10. Take them out of the oven and let the cakes cool slightly before turning them out of ther tins onto a cooling rack.

For the topping

  1. Whilst the cakes are cooling, whip the 250 ml of fresh cream.
  2. When the cakes are cool, pick any of them as the bottom layer and spead 2 1/2 tbsp of the jam on top.
  3. Add half of the whipped fresh cream on the bottom layer and spread evenly.
  4. Place the top layer on and repeat the process
  5. Add strawberries for decoration and enjoy!

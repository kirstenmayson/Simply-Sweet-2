The Reason Behind This

Trying to find delicious but simple recipes (that work) can be hard since there's so many websites and recipes out there. This site was created to help you easily find good recipes to treat and impress yourself, your family and maybe the few guests that you invite over. After baking cakes and desserts for so many years, I have finally come up with fail proof recipes that you can do without any adjustments needed to be made.

What to look forward to:

A selection of both cake and dessert recipes will be included. From rich chocolate squares to light and creamy Crème Brûlée. Some recipes will require a bit more time to make than others, but when you taste them, it will be well worth it! Welcome to the world of true decadence.

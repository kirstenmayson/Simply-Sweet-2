Yoghurt Dessert

Ingredients:

  • 150 gram packet of flavoured jelly(I like to use grape or strawberry)
  • 250 ml whipping fresh cream
  • 500 grams yoghurt (It's best to use a yoghurt flavour that is similar to the jelly)
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup cold water

For the topping

  • Strawberries or blueberries for garnishing

Recipe:

  1. Pour the contents of the jelly packet and half a cup of boiling water into a large bowl.
  2. Stir until the powder is almost desolved then add the 1 cup of cold water. Stir until fully desolved.
  3. Put the jelly in the fidge to cool but not congeal, it must just be cool and still in a liquid state.
  4. Beat the freah cream until stiff peaks start to form
  5. Once cooled take the jelly liquid out of the fridge.
  6. Add the yogurt to the jelly and mix with a spoon until well mixed
  7. Once completely mixed, add the fresh cream and fold it into the mixture with a whisk.
  8. Either leave the mixture in the bowl and set it in the fridge until it has a soft jelly like consistency, or put it into smaller dessert bowls.
  9. Preferably, let the dessert set over night.

For the topping

  • Add the strawberries once the dessert has set, or else the strawberries will just sink to the bottom of the bowl.

Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cups white granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups fresh cream
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 500 grams marscapone cheese at room temperature
  • 1/4 Cup strongly brewed coffee
  • 1/4 cup Amarula or Beileys Irish cream
  • 2 packets ladyfinger biscuits

For the topping

  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder

Recipe:

  1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until well blended.
  2. Whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils. Boil gently for 1 minute, remove from heat and allow to cool slightly.
  3. Cover the custard with cling wrap, making sure that you are putting it directly on the custard, and chill in refrigerator 1 hour.
  4. In a bowl, beat the cream with the vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  5. Take the custard mixture out of the fridge and remove the clingwrap. Whisk the mascarpone cheese into the custard mixture until smooth.
  6. In a small bowl, combine coffee and Amarula. Split ladyfingers in half lengthwise and dip them in the coffee mixture a second on each side.(Make sure you don't do it for too long or they will turn soggy and be too strong).
  7. Arrange half of soaked ladyfingers in bottom of a 30x15 centimetre dish. Spread half of mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers, then half of whipped cream over that. Repeat layers

For the topping

  1. Sprinkle the cocoa powder on top. Cover the dish with cling wrap and let it set in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours.
  2. Enjoy!
